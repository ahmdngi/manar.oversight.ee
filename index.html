    MaO offers the best products in the field of formwork and scaffolding, including H20 beam bridges and steel girders.

    In 1974, MAO (Salem Bin Hindi Al Harbi Establishment) secured its place in the country’s development and modernization journey by introducing

    Our core business includes industrial scaffolding services, structural concrete casting services (installation and dismantling),
