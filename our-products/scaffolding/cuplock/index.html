Cuplock

Cuplock

M-cuplock standards
1000 mm 1500 mm 2000 mm 2500 mm 3000 mm M-cuplock Standards are introduced in five basic sizes (1000 mm, 1500 mm, 2000 mm, 2500 mm, and 3000 mm) lengths. M-Cuplock Standards are manufactured from 48.3 mm O.D. Tube with 3.2mm thickness.
The lower cups are welded to standard at 500mm intervals. The upper cups (movable cups) are used to locking up to 4 ledgers at one node
The lowest bottom cup is welded at 80mm from the bottom end of the standard and the highest bottom cup is welded at 420 from the upper end of the standard.

 

M-Cuplock Ledgers

M-Cuplock Ledgers are used as the main horizontal connecting members for M-Cuplock system M-Cuplock Ledgers are introduced in large varieties to meet the requirements M-Cuplock Ledgers are manufactured from 48.3 mm O.D. Tube with 3.2mm thickness M-Cuplock Ledger ends are formed with circular profile and welded to ledger tube M-Cuplock Ledger ends meet with the bottom cup of the Standard and locked in place by the upper cup. Corresponding lock.

 

M-Cuplock Intermediate Transoms

M-Cuplock Intermediate Transoms are introduced in 7 sizes to fit design and site requirements M-Cuplock Intermediate Transoms are manufactured from 48.3mm . O.D. Tube with 3mm thicknessM-Cuplock Intermediate Transoms provide intermediate support to scaffold boards

M-Adjustable Base Jacks are made of steel plate, screw jack and steel handle M-Adjustable Base Jacks available in two types Hollow and solid
M-Adjustable Base Jacks provide method of adjustment for M-Cuplock structure. It fits directly into the M-Cuplock Standard.

 

M-Adjustable U-head jacks

M-Adjustable U-Head Jacks are made of a “U” shaped steel plate, screw jack and steel handle M-Adjustable U-Head Jacks are available in two types Hollow and solid. M-Adjustable U-Head Jacks are providing support for primary beams Traditional timber, steel, H20 beam and
aluminum beam. M-Adjustable U-Head Jacks are inserting into the top of the M-cuplock standards.

 

M-Base plate

M-Base Plate provides a flat support for M-Cuplock structure. M-Base Plate uses as simple support for M-Cuplock structure in case. of no need adjusting level.

 

M-Square Spigot

Used to join one M-Cuplock Standard to another coaxially. Bolt is placed ,transversely through the spigot and M-Cuplock Standard to prevent the spigot from pulling out of standard. Used to join one M-Cuplock Standard to another coaxially Bolt placed transversely through the Spigot and M-Cuplock Standard in case of lifting the M-Cuplock structure. In case of no lifting for M-Cuplock structure; don’t need to use bolts

M-Steel platform for scaffolding .M-Steel Plank lengths are available in 3 lengths suitable • for M-Cuplcok Ledger.

 

Scaffolding FIttings

Scaffold couplers are essentially the fundamental component that is used to assemble tube-and-coupler scaffolding. This basic fitting is designed to join two scaffold tubes that can be used to create a diverse range of scaffolding structures.
